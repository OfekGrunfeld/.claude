# Conversation Digest

## 1) Executive Summary

This conversation centered on building a practical, evidence-based iHerb supplement setup for muscle gain + fat loss, while avoiding overhyped products and unnecessary spending. The user asked about glutamine, omega-3, magnesium, vitamin D, iron, B12, caffeine, and what is actually needed in general. The discussion gradually narrowed toward a minimalist stack: creatine, vitamin D, omega-3, and optional pre-workout caffeine, with magnesium/B12/iron to be decided based on diet or bloodwork rather than bought blindly. The user also raised concerns about omega-3 oxidation, vitamin D deficiency risk from limited sun + olive skin, hair shedding, and whitening strips. A concrete purchase was made: creatine monohydrate, vitamin D3 2000 IU, caffeine 100 mg, NOW omega-3, Crest Whitestrips, and sensitive toothpaste. The main unresolved step is upcoming bloodwork to determine deficiencies and refine supplementation.
