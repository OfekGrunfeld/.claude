* **Recommended:** For sunscreen, prioritize **broad-spectrum SPF 30+ / ideally SPF 50**, with emphasis on **lightweight, non-greasy, invisible-feeling formulas**.
* **Recommended:** **Fluid / liquid sunscreen** was judged the best fit among Ultrasol formats for this skin type.
* **Recommended:** **CeraVe Foaming Cleanser** was treated as a sensible cleanser direction for normal-to-oily skin; this also appears in the attached note. 
* **Tentative:** **CeraVe Oil Control Gel Moisturizing Cream 52ml** was the main moisturizer direction when a face-specific option was discussed; this is also recorded in the attached note. 
* **Tentative:** A cheaper generic lotion could be acceptable if moisturizer is actually needed and tolerated, but this was not fully locked in.
* **Rejected:** Heavy / dry-skin-targeted moisturizers as default picks for this skin type.
* **Rejected:** Treating moisturizer as an automatically mandatory daily step.
* **Rejected:** Overcomplicated routines with many actives as the starting point.
