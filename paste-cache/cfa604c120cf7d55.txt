A few important notes from Claude Code docs:

CLAUDE.md works best when it is specific, concise, and well structured, and Anthropic suggests targeting under about 200 lines per file for better adherence.

Hooks live in ~/.claude/settings.json, so that is separate from your vault files; you only need hooks later if you want automation around notifications or workflow events.
